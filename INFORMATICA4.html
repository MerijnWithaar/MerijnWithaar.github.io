Andy Murray

Sir Andrew Murray is a British tennis player from Scotland. He has been a professional since 2005. He entered the top 100 for the first time in 2005, the top 50 for the first time in 2006 and the top 10 of the ATP rankings for the first time in 2007. Murray first reached first place on November 7, 2016.

  • He has won three grand slam titles
  • He is married to Kim Sears
  • He has three children, named Edie, Sophie and Teddy Murphy
